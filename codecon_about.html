About The Hackathon:

Neural Nexus Hackathon is an exhilarating event that brings together creative minds, coding enthusiasts, and tech-savvy individuals to collaborate, innovate, and push the boundaries of technology. Our hackathon is designed to provide a platform for participants to showcase their skills, and create transformative solutions using cutting-edge technologies.

Dates and Venue:

The Neural Nexus Hackathon will take place on the 7th of July 2023, 21:00 and end on 8th of July 2023, 21:00 providing participants with a dedicated 24 hours to immerse themselves in a world of coding challenges, networking opportunities, and groundbreaking projects.

Who Can Participate:

The Neural Nexus Hackathon welcomes participants from all backgrounds, whether you are an experienced developer, a passionate programmer, or a tech enthusiast eager to explore the world of coding. Our hackathon encourages diversity and fosters an inclusive environment for individuals to collaborate and learn from one another.

Themes and Tracks:

To provide a focused approach, Neural Nexus Hackathon offers various tracks and themes. Each track encompasses a specific domain, enabling participants to work on projects that align with their interests and expertise. Whether it's artificial intelligence, machine learning, Internet of Things (IoT), blockchain, or any other emerging technology, we have a track for you to dive into and unleash your creativity. More information on this will be shared later.

Prizes and Recognition:

At Neural Nexus Hackathon, we believe in celebrating innovation and rewarding exceptional talent. Throughout the event, our esteemed panel of judges will evaluate the projects based on creativity, technical prowess, and potential impact. The winning teams will have the chance to win exciting prizes, recognition from industry leaders, and an opportunity to showcase their projects to a wider audience.There will be a Prize Pool of 3000/- and could be extended if there are more registrations.

Join Neural Nexus Hackathon:

If you are passionate about technology, love coding challenges, and want to be part of an exciting community of innovators, Neural Nexus Hackathon is the perfect event for you. Don't miss this incredible opportunity to sharpen your skills, unleash your creativity, and make a lasting impact. Join us on 7th of July for an unforgettable hacking experience!
