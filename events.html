Machine Learning Session by Mr. Chirag Soni

22nd June 2023

Machine Learning Ideathon

5th May 2023

The club organized a Machine Learning Ideathon on 5 th May, 2023 that brought together students from different backgrounds to develop innovative solutions on various themes such as Fintech, E-Commerce Excursion, M2l2 (Manufacturing Mastery, Logistic Leap), Robotics Rampage, Cybersecurity Catalyst And Internet Of Ideas that allow to understand how technology can be used to drive innovation and growth.

With an enthusiastic response from all attendees so far, the Neural Nexus club is set to continue making strides in furthering knowledge and awareness of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.
